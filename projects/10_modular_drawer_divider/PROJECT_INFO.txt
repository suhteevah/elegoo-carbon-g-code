PROJECT: Modular Drawer Dividers
=================================
Category: Home & Living > Organization
Etsy Price: $12-18 (set of 6 mixed)

DESCRIPTION:
Interlocking cross-slot drawer dividers. Slot together to create custom
grid layouts. Three lengths for different drawer sizes. End tabs for grip.

FILES:
  divider_short_100mm.stl/gcode   - 100mm divider
  divider_medium_200mm.stl/gcode  - 200mm divider
  divider_long_300mm.stl/gcode    - 250mm (scaled to fit bed)

PRINT STATS:
  Short:   1h 03m | 15.1g PETG
  Medium:  1h 55m | 30.0g PETG
  Long:    1h 51m | 25.7g PETG
  Total:   4h 50m | 70.8g PETG (~$1.77 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print on side (standing up) for maximum rigidity
  - Low infill OK (15-20%)
  - Batch print multiples for efficiency
  - Slots slide together at cross points
  - Customize lengths in generate_all_projects.py

DIMENSIONS: 50mm tall, 2.5mm thick, lengths 100/200/250mm
