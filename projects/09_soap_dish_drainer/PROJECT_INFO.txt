PROJECT: Soap Dish Drainer
===========================
Category: Bath & Beauty > Soap Dishes
Etsy Price: $10-14

DESCRIPTION:
Angled soap dish with 8 drainage slats and front drain channel.
Keeps soap dry between uses. Elevated ribs allow airflow underneath soap.
