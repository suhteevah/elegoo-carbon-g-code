PROJECT: Minimalist Phone Stand
================================
Category: Office & Desk > Phone Accessories
Etsy Price: $12-18

DESCRIPTION:
Clean minimalist phone stand with 15-degree viewing angle. Built-in cable
channel for charging while docked. Fits all phone sizes.

FILES:
  phone_stand.stl/gcode - Single piece print

PRINT STATS:
  Time:     3h 25m
  Material: 52.8g PETG (~$1.32 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print upright (base on bed)
  - 20-30% infill for stability
  - Add rubber feet or silicone pads for grip
  - Cable channel runs through the front lip

DIMENSIONS: 80mm x 80mm base, 90mm tall back support
COMPATIBLE: All phones (portrait and landscape)
