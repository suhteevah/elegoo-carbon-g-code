PROJECT: Geometric Self-Watering Planter
========================================
Category: Home & Garden > Planters
Etsy Price: $18-25

DESCRIPTION:
Modern octagonal self-watering planter. Two-piece design: outer water reservoir
holds water, inner pot has 5 wicking drainage tubes that draw water up to roots.
Geometric/low-poly aesthetic. PETG makes it naturally water-resistant.

FILES:
  planter_reservoir.stl/gcode  - Outer reservoir (water tank)
  planter_inner_pot.stl/gcode  - Inner pot (with drain tubes)
  planter_combined_preview.stl - Both parts (preview only)
  generate_planter.py          - Parametric generator (customize dimensions)

PRINT STATS:
  Reservoir:  6h 21m | 82.5g PETG
  Inner Pot:  4h 12m | 64.5g PETG
  Total:     10h 33m | 147.0g PETG (~$3.68 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print reservoir with 100% bottom infill for watertight seal
  - PETG at 235C with 3+ walls recommended
  - No supports needed
  - Inner pot flange rests on reservoir lip
  - Consider food-safe filament if growing herbs

DIMENSIONS: 100mm diameter x 90mm tall (assembled)
