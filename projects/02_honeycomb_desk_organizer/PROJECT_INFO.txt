PROJECT: Honeycomb Desk Organizer
=================================
Category: Office & Desk > Organizers
Etsy Price: $15-22

DESCRIPTION:
Triple hexagonal cell desk organizer. Three connected honeycomb cups for pens,
pencils, scissors, markers. Structural bridges between cells. Geometric aesthetic.

FILES:
  honeycomb_organizer.stl/gcode - Single print, all 3 cells

PRINT STATS:
  Time:     9h 34m
  Material: 114.2g PETG (~$2.85 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print upright (cells open at top)
  - 20% infill is sufficient
  - No supports needed
  - Looks great in two-tone filament

DIMENSIONS: ~100mm wide x 100mm deep x 100mm tall
