PROJECT: Geometric Tea Light Holder
=====================================
Category: Home & Living > Candle Holders
Etsy Price: $10-15

DESCRIPTION:
12-sided faceted tea light holder with alternating rotated layers for a
mesmerizing crystalline pattern. Fits standard 38mm tea lights.
Decorative exterior columns add visual interest.

FILES:
  tea_light_holder.stl/gcode - Single piece

PRINT STATS:
  Time:     3h 38m
  Material: 54.7g PETG (~$1.37 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print upright (open top)
  - No supports needed
  - The faceted layers create beautiful light patterns
  - PETG is heat-safe for tea lights (higher melting point than PLA)
  - Translucent filament creates amazing glow-through effect

DIMENSIONS: 70mm diameter x 50mm tall
FITS: Standard tea light candles (38mm diameter)
SAFETY: Use PETG or ABS only - PLA can deform near flames
