PROJECT: Floating Shelf Bracket Set
=====================================

Hidden-mount L-bracket shelf supports with diagonal brace for strength.
Two sizes: 150mm and 200mm shelf depth. Screw-mount to wall studs.

FILES:
  shelf_bracket_small_150mm.stl/gcode - 150mm depth pair
  shelf_bracket_large_200mm.stl/gcode - 200mm depth pair

PRINT STATS:
  Small:   2h 56m | 40.1g PETG
  Large:   3h 22m | 49.7g PETG
  Total:   6h 18m | 89.8g PETG (~$2.25 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print on side for maximum layer adhesion along stress lines
  - 50%+ infill for load-bearing use
  - PETG or ABS recommended (NOT PLA for structural)
  - Mount to wall studs, not just drywall
  - Print 2x each for a matching pair

WEIGHT CAPACITY: 5-10kg per pair (depending on mounting)
