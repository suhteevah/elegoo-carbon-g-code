PROJECT: Modern Wall Hook Set
==============================
Category: Home & Living > Hooks & Hardware
Etsy Price: $12-18 (set of 3)

DESCRIPTION:
Set of 3 modern J-shaped wall hooks in small, medium, and large sizes.
Clean minimalist design for coats, bags, towels, keys. Screw-mount.

FILES:
  wall_hook_small.stl/gcode   - 30mm hook (keys, light items)
  wall_hook_medium.stl/gcode  - 45mm hook (bags, towels)
  wall_hook_large.stl/gcode   - 60mm hook (coats, heavy items)

PRINT STATS:
  Small:   1h 19m |  7.7g PETG
  Medium:  1h 41m | 13.4g PETG
  Large:   2h 04m | 20.7g PETG
  Total:   5h 04m | 41.7g PETG (~$1.04 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print flat (back plate on bed) for maximum strength
  - 40%+ infill recommended for load-bearing strength
  - PETG preferred over PLA for durability
  - Use wall anchors for drywall mounting

WEIGHT CAPACITY: ~2-5kg depending on size and mounting
