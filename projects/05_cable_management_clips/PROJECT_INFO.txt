PROJECT: Cable Management Clips
================================
Category: Office & Desk > Cable Management
Etsy Price: $8-12 (set of 6 mixed)

DESCRIPTION:
Adhesive-back cable management clips in 1, 2, and 3-slot variants.
Keep desk cables organized. Flat base for 3M adhesive tape mounting.

FILES:
  cable_clip_1slot.stl/gcode  - Single cable (USB, Lightning)
  cable_clip_2slot.stl/gcode  - Two cables
  cable_clip_3slot.stl/gcode  - Three cables

PRINT STATS:
  1-slot:    20m | 2.2g PETG
  2-slot:    26m | 3.6g PETG
  3-slot:    29m | 5.0g PETG
  Total:   1h15m | 10.7g PETG (~$0.27 filament)

PRINT TIPS:
  - Print flat (base down)
  - Low infill OK (15%)
  - Very fast prints - batch multiple on plate
  - Attach with 3M VHB tape or Command strips
  - Sell in packs of 6-10 for best Etsy value

CABLE DIAMETER: ~8mm slot (fits most USB/Lightning/Type-C cables)
